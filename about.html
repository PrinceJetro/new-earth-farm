Our Story

New Earth Farms was founded with a simple but powerful vision — to nourish people and communities through sustainable and ethical farming. From small beginnings, we have grown into a diverse farm specializing in poultry, fisheries, piggery, and crop cultivation.

Every product we offer is a reflection of our commitment to quality, care, and the environment. We believe in farming that respects the land while delivering exceptional products to our community.

"Farming is a profession of hope, and at New Earth Farms, we're planting seeds for a better tomorrow." - Davis Sanni

What We Stand For

Quality

We prioritize the health and wellbeing of our animals and crops to deliver superior products that meet the highest standards.

Sustainability

We farm with respect for nature, implementing practices that ensure future generations can thrive on healthy land.

Community

Building strong relationships with our customers and supporting local communities is at the heart of what we do.

Innovation

We embrace modern, responsible farming techniques to enhance productivity while caring for our environment.

What We Offer

Poultry Farming

Healthy, well-cared-for birds raised for quality eggs and meat in free-range environments that prioritize animal welfare.

Fisheries

Fresh fish grown sustainably with attention to water quality and healthy breeding practices for premium aquaculture products.

Piggery

Carefully managed pigs raised for premium pork production in clean, healthy environments with ethical treatment standards.

Crop Farming

Nutritious crops grown with environmentally friendly methods, without compromising on quality or nutritional value.
